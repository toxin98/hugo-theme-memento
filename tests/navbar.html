The Invisible Navigation

Notice how the page content respects the header spacing without polluting the body tag. We applied padding-top directly to the main element.

As you scroll down, the secondary navigation bar will slide up and hide behind the "New Yorker" logo. The logo itself remains steadfastly anchored to the top of the viewport, ensuring brand visibility at all times.

This dual-layer approach uses CSS z-index stacking contexts effectively. The white background of the logo bar acts as an occluding layer for the navigation bar beneath it.
